Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 15, 2026

CardBrain is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect card numbers, bank credentials, personal finance account data, usage analytics, or tracking data. This policy explains how the app works so you can use it with confidence.

What We Collect

CardBrain does not require an account or sign-in. Your wallet selections, benefit tracking progress, notes, and preferences stay on your device. Optional purchases and feedback are handled as described below.

Local Storage

Your selected cards, benefit tracking progress, notes, and preferences are stored locally on your device using iOS built-in storage (UserDefaults). This wallet data is not sent to us.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed.

Third-Party Services

CardBrain uses a minimal set of third-party services:

  • Apple StoreKit and RevenueCat (Pro purchases): If you choose to purchase CardBrain Pro, payment is handled by Apple through the App Store and purchase entitlement status is managed through RevenueCat. We do not see or store your payment card information. Apple's privacy policy governs App Store payments: apple.com/legal/privacy.
  • Public Card Catalog and Images: The app may download public card benefit data and mirrored card images from our GitHub Pages site over HTTPS. These requests are used to keep supported cards and benefits up to date. No personal card selections, benefit progress, notes, or other user-entered data is sent in these requests.

CardBrain does not use advertising SDKs or third-party analytics tools.

Feedback

If you choose to send feedback in the app, your message, optional email address, and optional diagnostic context such as app version, device model, OS version, and wallet card count are submitted through Formspree so we can read and respond to your message. Do not include card numbers or other sensitive financial information in feedback.

Children's Privacy

CardBrain does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or anything else about CardBrain, you can reach us at emiandtuzi@gmail.com.
